Puetro Rican Music


“DeBÍ TiRAR MáS FOToS,” was officially released on January 5, 2025, is one of Bad Bunny’s most personal albums.
This new album blends together all kinds of traditional Puerto Rican music types, some of the main ones being Reggaetón, Salsa, Boleros , and many more.
Bad bunny uses the lyrics for each song to reflect Puerto Ricos heritage and some of the issues it still has even up until today.
