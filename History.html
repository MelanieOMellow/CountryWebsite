Castillo San Felipe del Morrois

Castillo San Felipe del Morrois was built in 1539-1790
is a beautiful 16th-century fortress that is located in Old San Juan, Puerto ricos capital.
it was built for the sole purpose of protecting the city from boat or water related attacks.
In its time of being used it stopped many attacks including a very significant attack from Sir Francis Drake
it had military forces and was a military base until 1961.


Castillo San Cristóbal

Castillo San Cristóbal created in 1766 - 1783
is an old but still impressive fortress in the Old San Juan historic quarter of San Juan,
its mostly known for being the largest building created by the Spanish in the modern day.
It was built for the purpose of protecting the city of San Juan from attack by land from east attacks.
